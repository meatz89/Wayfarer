# Phase 4 Session 8: "Make 50 Coins in 14 Days" Economic Analysis

## Challenge Overview

**Starting Conditions**:
- Location: Millbrook
- Starting capital: 12 coins
- Starting equipment: Trade Tools
- Inventory: 4 base slots
- Target: 50 coins by end of Day 14
- **Net profit needed**: 38 coins over 14 days = ~2.7 coins per day average

## Strategy A: Equipment Master Approach

### Equipment Investment Plan
**Days 1-3**: Earn equipment funds through contracts
- Available contracts: Standard (8 coins, 3 days), Craft (12 coins, 2 days, requires Trade Tools)
- Day 1: Accept Craft contract (12 coins) - requires 2 days work
- Day 2: Complete Craft contract (+12 coins, total: 24 coins)
- Day 3: Rest or simple trading

**Days 4-5**: Equipment commissioning
- Commission Climbing Gear: 5 coins + 1 day (total: 19 coins, all routes unlocked)
- Commission Navigation Tools: 5 coins + 1 day (total: 14 coins, weather independence)

**Days 6-14**: Maximize route access for optimal opportunities
- Access to all 8 routes enables:
  - Rush contracts (15 coins, 1 day) requiring specific routes
  - Exploration contracts (6 coins + discovery bonus)
  - Optimal trade circuit selection

**Economic Projection**:
- Equipment cost: 10 coins
- Remaining capital after equipment: 14 coins
- Available days for optimization: 9 days
- Required daily profit: (50-14)/9 = 4 coins per day
- **Viability**: ✅ Rush contracts (15 coins/day) and optimal trade circuits make this achievable

## Strategy B: Trade Optimization Approach

### Cargo Capacity Maximization
**Days 1-2**: Acquire cart transport access
- Use starting 12 coins for initial trading capital
- Cart provides +3 slots (7 total capacity)
- Limited to 3 cart routes but maximum cargo efficiency

**High-Volume Trading Analysis**:
**Best cargo mix with 7 slots**:
- 2 Lumber (4 slots): Buy 8 coins, sell 16 coins = 8 profit
- 3 Herbs (3 slots): Buy 9 coins, sell 18 coins = 9 profit
- **Total per trip**: 17 coins investment, 34 coins return = 17 profit per trip

**Route efficiency**: 
- Cart routes require 2 periods each
- Can complete 1 full trade circuit per day (4 periods travel + work)
- **Daily profit potential**: 17 coins per trip, 1 trip per day

**Economic Projection**:
- Day 1-2: Build up to 17 coin investment capacity
- Days 3-14: 12 days × 17 coins profit = 204 coins theoretical
- **Practical constraints**: Stamina, market availability, competition
- **Required achievement**: Only need 3 successful trips = 51 coins
- **Viability**: ✅ Easily achievable with buffer for setbacks

## Strategy C: Contract Specialization Approach

### Renewable Contract Focus
**Contract Income Analysis**:
- Rush contracts: 15 coins, 1 day (require equipment)
- Standard contracts: 8 coins, 3 days (reliable)
- Craft contracts: 12 coins, 2 days (require Trade Tools)
- Exploration contracts: 6 coins + bonus, 5 days

**Mixed approach calculation**:
- Week 1: 2 Craft contracts (24 coins) + 1 Standard contract (8 coins) = 32 coins
- Week 2: 2 Rush contracts (30 coins) + trading = 30+ coins
- **Total potential**: 62+ coins

**Economic Projection**:
- Equipment investment: 5-10 coins for specific contracts
- Reliable income stream through renewable contracts
- Lower risk than pure trading approach
- **Viability**: ✅ Multiple paths to 50+ coins

## Trade-off Analysis

### Equipment vs Capital Trade-offs
**Equipment Master**:
- ✅ Pros: Access to all opportunities, maximum flexibility
- ❌ Cons: High upfront investment, slower early income

**Trade Optimization**:
- ✅ Pros: High profit margins, predictable income
- ❌ Cons: Route limitations, inventory constraints

**Contract Specialization**:
- ✅ Pros: Steady income, lower risk, scalable
- ❌ Cons: Depends on NPC availability, time pressure

### Mathematical Constraint Validation

**Inventory Pressure Confirmed**:
- Optimal cargo (2 Lumber + 3 Herbs) = 7 slots needed
- Base inventory: 4 slots available
- Cart bonus: +3 slots but blocks 5 of 8 routes
- **Cannot maximize both cargo AND route access** ✅

**Time Pressure Confirmed**:
- Rush contracts: 1 day deadline creates urgency
- Equipment commissioning: Consumes valuable time blocks
- Travel time: 1-2 periods per route reduces available work time
- **Cannot do everything without prioritization** ✅

**Economic Pressure Confirmed**:
- Equipment costs: 15 coins total for full loadout
- Starting capital: 12 coins
- Must choose between immediate trading and future capability
- **Resource allocation forces strategic decisions** ✅
