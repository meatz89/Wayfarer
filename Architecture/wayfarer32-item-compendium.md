# Wayfarer: Item Categorization System

## TOOLS (Enabling Actions)

### Travel Tools
1. **Sturdy Boots** - Reduces Energy cost of travel by 1
2. **Walking Staff** - Grants +1 to Physical tests on difficult terrain
3. **Traveler's Map** - Reveals paths between known locations
4. **Compass** - Prevents becoming lost when traveling unfamiliar routes
5. **Oilskin Cloak** - Prevents Energy penalty during rain or snow

### Scholarly Tools
1. **Writing Kit** - Enables note-taking and correspondence
2. **Reading Spectacles** - Grants +1 to Observation for reading and detailed study
3. **Abacus** - Reduces AP cost of complex calculations by 1
4. **Journal** - Automatically records important discoveries
5. **Magnifying Glass** - Reveals tiny details invisible to naked eye

### Professional Tools
1. **Merchant's Scale** - Ensures fair weight measurement in transactions
2. **Artisan's Tools** - Specialized equipment for a specific craft
3. **Lockpicks** - Opens locks without proper key
4. **Climbing Gear** - Enables scaling difficult surfaces
5. **Disguise Kit** - Allows changing appearance temporarily

## ITEMS (Persistent Objects)

### Clothing
1. **Fur-Lined Gloves** - Prevents dexterity penalties in cold weather
2. **Heavy Cloak** - Reduces Energy cost in cold environments
3. **Pilgrim's Robes** - Improves initial impression with religious figures
4. **Merchant's Attire** - Improves initial impression with traders
5. **Fine Clothing** - Improves initial impression with nobility

### Containers
1. **Traveler's Pack** - Increases basic carrying capacity
2. **Scholar's Satchel** - Protects books and documents from damage
3. **Medicine Pouch** - Maintains potency of medical items

### Valuables
1. **Silver Brooch** - Can be traded for currency or presented as gift
2. **Gold Ring** - Improves impression with merchants and nobility
3. **Foreign Coins** - Can be traded or used to start conversations
4. **Gemstone** - Can be sold for significant currency
5. **Fine Art Object** - Appreciated by collectors and the cultured

## CONSUMABLES (Single-Use Items)

### Food
1. **Trail Rations** - Basic preserved food (-3 Hunger)
2. **Preserved Meat** - Protein-rich sustenance (-6 Hunger)
3. **Traveler's Bread** - Dense, long-lasting nutrients (-4 Hunger)
4. **Dried Fruit** - Sweet energy source (-2 Hunger, -1 Mental Strain)
5. **Innkeeper's Stew** - Hot, freshly prepared meal (-8 Hunger)

### Drink
1. **Clean Water** - Basic hydration
2. **Herbal Tea** - Mild refreshment (-1 Mental Strain)
3. **Ale or Wine** - Social lubricant (-2 Isolation when shared)
4. **Hot Broth** - Warming drink for cold weather (+1 Energy in cold)
5. **Strong Spirits** - Potent drink (+2 Vigor temporarily)

### Medicine
1. **Healing Salve** - Treats minor wounds (+1 Health)
2. **Fever Tonic** - Reduces symptoms of illness (-2 Exhaustion)
3. **Pain Remedy** - Alleviates pain from injuries (-3 Exhaustion)
4. **Sleeping Draught** - Ensures restful sleep (+2 to rest quality)
5. **Purifying Tincture** - Treats poisoning or contamination

### Herbs
1. **Dried Mint** - Calming herb (-1 Mental Strain)
2. **Willow Bark** - Pain-reducing herb (-1 Exhaustion)
3. **Valerian Root** - Sleep-inducing herb (+1 to rest quality)
4. **Garlic Clove** - Disease-resistant properties (prevents illness)
5. **Sage Leaves** - Clarity-enhancing herb (+1 to mental skills temporarily)

### Stimulants
1. **Strong Tea** - Mild stimulant (-1 Exhaustion temporarily)
2. **Bitter Root** - Energy-boosting herb (-2 Exhaustion temporarily)
3. **Spiced Coffee** - Potent alertness booster (+1 AP for next action)
4. **Stamina Powder** - Rare stimulant (-3 Exhaustion temporarily)
5. **Focus Tonic** - Concentration enhancer (+1 to Observation temporarily)

### Relaxants
1. **Calming Tea** - Mild relaxant (-2 Mental Strain)
2. **Lavender Bundle** - Improves sleep quality (+1 to rest benefits)
3. **Meditation Incense** - Aids concentration (-3 Mental Strain during use)
4. **Muscle Salve** - Soothes physical fatigue (-2 Exhaustion)
5. **Comfort Draught** - Potent relaxant (-4 Mental Strain)

This categorization system balances elegance with meaningful mechanical impacts. Each item has a clear purpose tied directly to game systems, while maintaining the verisimilitude of a medieval setting. The effects are straightforward enough for easy implementation but diverse enough to create interesting tactical choices for players.
